Descriptions

The complex located on the southeast side of Crete, next to the Chrysoskalitissa Monastery and a few steps before the marvelous Elafonissi beach, is the newly built villa complex.The Villas are built at the southwestern most end of the Chania Prefecture.

The space These 850 – 950 sq. ft luxury villas are divided into two levels with two bedrooms, two bathrooms and a central space which includes a fully equipped kitchen and a living room with a fireplace.

They are designed to accommodate two to five persons and provide all the modern comforts (refrigerator, oven, coffee machine, toaster, hair dryer, satellite TV, air-conditioning, fireplace, etc.)

Each villa is tastefully decorated throughout to create a comfortable, relaxing, and pleasant stay. The private spacious balconies provide the perfect spot to enjoy the infinite and spectacular views throughout the day and night.

Within the Villa complex is a large pool.
